习题取材于国际中文教材《跨越丝路》1–4 册——阿联酋教育部“中文百校项目”C2–C3 阶段的指定教材，依据最新 2025–2026 版 CCG（Comprehensive Curriculum Guide，阿联酋教育部课程司发布的科目教学综合指南）与新 HSK 3.0 标准编写，科学分级、循序渐进。

These exercises are drawn from the international Chinese-language series Across the Silk Road (《跨越丝路》), Books 1–4 — the prescribed textbook for stages C2–C3 of the UAE Ministry of Education’s “Chinese in 100 Schools” programme (中文百校项目). They are developed in accordance with the latest 2025–2026 Comprehensive Curriculum Guide (CCG) — the official subject-teaching framework issued by the Curriculum Department of the UAE Ministry of Education — and the new HSK 3.0 standard, ensuring a rigorous, level-by-level progression.
What is HSK 3.0?  HSK (Hànyǔ Shuǐpíng Kǎoshì) is the official international standardised test of Chinese-language proficiency, administered under the Ministry of Education of China. HSK 3.0 is its latest framework (2021) — the Chinese Proficiency Grading Standards for International Chinese Language Education — organising proficiency into 3 stages and 9 levels spanning vocabulary, grammar, characters and communicative skills.
HSK（汉语水平考试）是中国教育部主管的国际中文水平标准化考试;HSK 3.0 为其 2021 年最新标准《国际中文教育中文水平等级标准》,分三级九等。

三层进阶，精准分层
A three-tier progression, precisely differentiated
展开详情 Details▸
Tier 1 · 迈向掌握：聚焦《跨越丝路》Level 1–9 的基础知识（核心词汇与句型）。
Tier 2 · 达到掌握：基础知识 + HSK 3.0；其中 Level 3 及以上增加一篇阅读理解。
Tier 3 · 深度掌握：基础知识 + HSK 3.0 + 阅读理解 + 开放性与批判性思维题，围绕中阿文化与习俗的了解和对比。其中 Level 1–4 的批判性思维题分为 C2、C3 两个等级。
  • Tier 1 · Building toward mastery — focuses on the core knowledge (key vocabulary and sentence patterns) of Across the Silk Road, Levels 1–9.
  • Tier 2 · Reaching mastery — core knowledge plus HSK 3.0 practice; from Level 3 onward, a reading-comprehension passage is added.
  • Tier 3 · Mastery in depth — core knowledge, HSK 3.0, reading comprehension, and open-ended and critical-thinking tasks that explore and compare Chinese and Emirati culture and customs. For Levels 1–4, the critical-thinking tasks are further divided into two grades, C2 and C3.
💡 友情提示：本平台 Level 1–8 的分级，是 2028 年以后执行的标准分级。
